Alex is a multimedia producer specializing in short-form documentary and narrative video.

Telling Your Story

In addition to producing independent projects, I partner with individuals and organizations to create videos that give their viewers singular insight into who they are and what they do.

It’s a creative approach to the storytelling process. By combining documentary and narrative elements, we create compelling portraits that look and feel a little different than the typical promotional video.

Whether you know your story already, or still need help finding it, we’ll bring it to life in ways that are both unexpected and distinct.
